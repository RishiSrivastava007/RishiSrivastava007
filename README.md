## 📁 Featured Projects

### 🚨 Smart Pothole & Road Condition Monitoring System
> An AI-driven municipal maintenance solution that automatically detects road hazards and maps them.
- **Key Features:** Detects road anomalies and potholes from user-uploaded images/video using a custom-trained **YOLOv8** & **OpenCV** model, then maps precise geolocations onto an interactive live dashboard.
- **Tech Stack:** `React.js`, `Express.js`, `Node.js`, `MongoDB`, `Python`, `YOLOv8`, `OpenCV`, `Leaflet Maps`

### ♻️ FoodShare AI – Surplus Marketplace & Presentation Simulator
> A high-performance interactive simulation platform mapping surplus food redistribution routes.
- **Key Features:** Computes exact geodesic circle distances between food donors and recipient NGOs using the **Haversine formula**, maps real-time dispatch routes, triggers emergency expiry alerts, and validates pick-ups via a secure **QR handshake mechanism**.
- **Tech Stack:** `HTML5`, `CSS3`, `JavaScript (ES6)`, `Leaflet.js`, `LocalStorage Persistence`

### 📄 AI Invoice Generator
> An automated, intelligent billing enrichment and processing backend engine.
- **Key Features:** Integrates **Google Gemini AI** to auto-format, translate, and enrich metadata from raw purchase invoices before storing them securely.
- **Tech Stack:** `Node.js`, `Express.js`, `MongoDB`, `Google Gemini AI APIs`
